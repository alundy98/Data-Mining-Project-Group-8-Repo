Data Mining Project Team 8 - Alec Lundy, AJ Clark

Project: Wellness Grocery Inventory Analysis
Objective:
Your mission is to analyze the nutritional composition of the store’s inventory to find patterns in the items they sell. This analysis will help the business assess if their current offerings align with their "Wellness and Health" ethos.

The project follows a comprehensive unsupervised learning pipeline, including data normalization, dimensionality reduction (PCA), clustering (k-Means), and cluster profiling. Strong collaboration and documentation established in Week 1 are critical for translating technical clusters into actionable business insights.
